Welcome to the official Beneque website!


You can provide an sentence/phrase in English, and we will translate it to Beneque.

Beneque is not a fully developed language and if your English sentence/phrase contains a word that we have not defined in Beneque, the Beneque translation for that word will just be the original English word.

If you put more than one character of punctionation in a row (eg. "!!!!"), the Beneque version will only be one instance of that character in that spot (eg. "!")
